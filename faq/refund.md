# Refund Policy

## Eligibility
You may return unused and undamaged items within 30 days of receipt for a full refund.

## Timeframe
Once we receive your return, processing takes 3-5 business days. The refund will be credited back to your original payment method.

## How to Initiate a Refund
You can initiate a refund by asking our Support Assistant to process a return for your order number, or by visiting the "Returns" section in your account.
