# Shipping Policy

## Domestic Shipping
All domestic orders take 3-5 business days to process. Standard shipping takes an additional 2-4 days.

## International Shipping
International shipping can take anywhere from 7-14 business days depending on customs processing in your country.

## Tracking
Once your order is shipped, you will receive a tracking number via email. You can also track your shipment by providing your order number to our Support Assistant.
